## Variables

### Identifiers

**subject** (numeric) - The ID of the studied subject.

**activity** (character) - The type of activity performed when measurements was taken.

 *Note - subject is of type numeric, while activity is of type character.
 
 
### Activity Labels

Activities performed by the subject while the measure was taken. 

1. Walking
2. Walking upstairs
3. Walking downstairs
4. Sitting
5. Standing
6. Laying
